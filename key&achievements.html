Key Achievements:

NLR strives to work and coordinate activities consistently with other strategic partners or international organizations. NLR is a member organization of the International Federation of Anti- Leprosy Associations (ILEP). The disease control activities are carried out directly through the Ministry of Health and Population.

  • a. Leprosy elimination target at National & Regional Level reached.
  • b. Disabilities due to leprosy reduced.
  • c. Mainstreamed disability due to leprosy to general disabilities.
  • d. Functional and sustainable self-help groups established at local and province level.
  • e. Innovative approaches generated executed and publicized for replication such as Leprosy Post-Exposure Prophylaxis (LPEP), Our Village Model Village (OVMV), Inspire to Care (I2C).
  • f. For the first time in Nepal, NLR Nepal introduced Outcome Mapping (OM) as PME tools in its program I2C).
